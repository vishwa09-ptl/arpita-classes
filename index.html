Reading

FAQs

Questions? We've
Got Answers

Everything you need to know before you join. Can't find your answer? Call us directly at +91 99988 55440.

How many months does the IELTS course take?
Our standard IELTS program runs for 2–3 months depending on your current level and target band. We also offer intensive 4-week crash courses for students with upcoming exam dates. A free level assessment is done before enrollment.
Do you offer online IELTS coaching?
Yes! All three of our programs — IELTS Coaching, Spoken English, and Phonics for Kids — are available in fully live online mode via video conferencing. Online students receive the same study material and feedback as offline students.
What is the ideal age to start Phonics?
The ideal age to begin phonics is between 3 and 5 years when children are most receptive to sound-letter associations. However, our program accepts children up to age 10. Early starters tend to develop stronger reading skills and spelling abilities throughout school.
Can I take a free demo class before enrolling?
Absolutely. We offer a free, no-obligation demo class for all our programs. Fill in the contact form on this page or call us directly, and we will schedule your demo at a convenient time. There is no pressure to join after the demo.
What IELTS band score can I expect to achieve?
Our students consistently achieve scores between Band 6 and Band 7.5. Results depend on your current proficiency, study effort, and time invested. We do a free assessment at the start to set realistic targets and build a personalised improvement plan.
Do you provide study materials and practice tests?
Yes, all study materials are included in the course fee — printed and digital worksheets, vocabulary lists, grammar guides, speaking topic banks, and full-length mock test papers. For IELTS students, we conduct at least 4 full mock tests with detailed band-wise analysis.
Is Spoken English useful for working professionals?
Absolutely. Our Spoken English course is specifically designed with working professionals in mind. We cover business communication, email writing, presentation delivery, and interview preparation — skills that directly impact career growth.
How do I enrol or get more information?
You can enquire by filling in the contact form on this page, calling us at +91 99988 55440, or messaging us on WhatsApp. We will get back to you within a few hours to discuss your goals, answer your questions, and schedule a free demo class.
